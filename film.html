Peculiar Emagination
Medium: Adobe Premier Pro, Film

“Peculiar Emagination” because of the fact that these types of thoughts of suicide and depression are among everyone, but at the same time, it feels as though no one can speak about how they feel. Because in a sense everyone’s symptoms are different and theoretical. It wasn’t a goodbye like I hoped for it to be, but because of how theoretical I became when creating it. It gained its name “Peculiar Emagination” because it represented how I felt on the inside which was very indifferent from what I showed on the outside. You could almost say in a sense this could have been a video diary.
