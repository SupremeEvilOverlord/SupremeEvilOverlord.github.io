Alexis Lienhard

Digital Media Artist

Artist Statement

My work explores the relationship between trauma and emotional memories. With influences as diverse as Blake and Francis Bacon, new synergies are distilled from both orderly and random narratives. Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated by the unrelenting divergence of the mind. What starts out as vision soon becomes debased into a cacophony of fear, leaving only a sense of chaos and the chance of a new order. As shimmering forms become clarified through studious and personal practice, the viewer is left with a testament to the possibilities of our future.

About me

I am from Roseville, California but I go to school at San Jose State University as a Digital Media Art major.
I have experience using adobe products like photoshop, lightroom, illustrator, dimensions, and premier pro! I heavily use Github for verison control, as well as processing, brackets, and more for my web formats.
