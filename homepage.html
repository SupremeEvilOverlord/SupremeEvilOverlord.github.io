Artist Statement

We all hide something about ourselves, I create art to bring light into the truth and meaning of our everyday lives. My work explores the relationship between trauma, emotions, and humanity. As trauma comes in different forms that cannot always be told in the same way; slowly the emotional connections between humans change, as people only show how they act versus how they internally feel; by doing this we as humans become desensitized to our emotions and we slowly start to lose the uniqueness of what made us who we really are as humans.
Each piece of artwork tells a story of its own part of my life. My Graphic design work is a portrayal of my life growing up and how I wanted to feel. The need to be perfect brought out the core basics of who I was on the outside. A girl from the valley, trying to change the perspective of how people see life and the emotions in it.
My new media Artwork portrays the loss of social interactions from a false reality we create for ourselves, with artificial intelligence. It shows how we use it to the point where it can be addictive, dangerous and lead to our downfall. My photography speaks levels upon the trauma I have experienced in my life, but haven’t spoken out about. “Uncovering the Self” shows the trauma that is taboo and you cannot speak about. While my photo work in “GoodBye” speaks of many forms of mental illnesses that everyday people deal with daily but are afraid to speak out about. These people are afraid of opening up, seeking help terrified of the after-effects of how people may treat them. The film portion goes deeper into depth when these feelings of trauma continue to be hidden and eventually get out of control. Fine-art of my emotions towards others, by allowing myself to express my outer emotions towards others. By creating the wire heart, that is open and has color throughout, reminds us of the beauty and energy that flows in something so precious to keep us alive. The crossbow while it may be made out of cardboard and gives the illusion of something that may be weak, it's actually the opposite and it’s used to protect the heart.
My art is for the people who feel unheard, who want to change the perspective. Who wants to tell people to stop judging a book by its cover. Get in there and read the entire story.
Let’s change the perspective of how we see life. What keeps me coming back to the studio, the sketchbook, the laptop is to tell a story for those who are misunderstood and unheard in this society, who see the world in a different way from others.
My viewers' reaction once they see each piece of my work is pure fascination. They are surprised by what I think and feel in my artwork. The best way to describe my artwork is as one person said “These were hard for me to look at, but this is so necessary to see”, making me know that my viewers and critics understand the trauma, the emotion, and the humanity of my artwork without a lot of explanations. So why don’t we change the perspective by bringing light into the truth and meaning of our everyday lives, we need to be set free and release ourselves from the fears that hold us back.

I have experience using Adobe products like Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, Dimensions, and Premier Pro! I heavily use Github for verison control, as well as Processing, Brackets, and more for my web formats.
