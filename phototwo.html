Uncovering the Self


Portrait Photography

Adobe Lightroom, Canon Camera

This project was based on two halves of emotions, which would be the inside and outside of a person. So instead of using the traditional approach of putting my outside personality into the picture, I chose to bring out my inside personality. These photos humanize my fears and doubts that came from having a learning disability, how they are always watching and hurting me.
