Fine Art

The styles of fine art I have done.

Never see never will

3D-Concepts
Materials: CDs, Crayola Crans, Blue tape, Clear Tape, white paper

Never see never will an art project inspired by David Brooks and his art project Never see never will. Knowing that’d I’d never get to see the dinosaurs get hit by the asteroid I created my faint memories of seeing the depictions in the books and CDs.

Carboard Sculpture

3D-Concepts
Materials: Wire, Cardboard, Blue Tape, Clear Tape, and Hot Glue.

Bringing my memories of play, back to life when I was a child I always wanted to shoot a bow and arrow, but I never got to shoot one until the pandemic happened. So I created a fully functional crossbow as a way to challenge my skills as well as keep me entertained.

Wire Sculpture

3D-Concepts
Materials: Wire, Metal Peices, Red Paint, Wooden Heart

Challenged to recreate an object by wire, I chose the heart. With the central wisdom of feeling as opposed to the head-wisdom of the reason being in such a small form enlarging the size to bring out the pure importance of the heart.
