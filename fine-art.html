Never see never will

3D-Concepts
Materials: CDs, Crayola Crans, Blue tape, Clear Tape, white paper

These materials helped me try to recreate the dinosaurs getting hit by an asteroid. Because I have never seen a dinosaur or an asteroid in my life like most people and when I have it was usually through books and videos I thought it would be great to incorporate that into it. The drawings are not that detailed nor can you really see them on the CD, but the past is like a lightened memory. So when it comes through the picture and onto a solid surface you see the turmoil of the dinosaurs and the explosion of the asteroid hitting the earth.
