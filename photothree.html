Black and White Photography

This project was based on mental illness and how it is portrayed inside the minds of the beholder, as well as the effects of this illness and how it can sometimes proceed to death in some or most cases at times. The illnesses that are being portrayed are anxiety, schizophrenia, depression, as for death, there will depictions of self-harm, suicide, and symbols of death in fiction.
